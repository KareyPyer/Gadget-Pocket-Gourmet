# 🌿 Wasabi Powder (粉わさび)

> *“A dormant volcano you activate with water.” — Dr Umami*

---

## 🧬 Identity

- **Japanese Name:** 粉わさび
- **Romanization:** Kona wasabi / Wasabi powder
- **Category:** Spice / Condiment
- **Origin:** Japan (modern processing of wasabi root)
- **Flavor Profile:** Sharp, pungent, sinus-activating heat, slightly herbal

---

## 🧪 Description

Wasabi powder is a dehydrated form of wasabi, made by grinding and drying either real wasabi root or more commonly a mixture of horseradish, mustard, and green coloring.

When mixed with water, it reactivates its signature pungent compounds.

Dr Umami classifies it as:

> “A chemical alarm system for the human taste experience.”

---

## 🍜 Traditional Uses

- Sushi accompaniment 🍣
- Sashimi enhancement 🐟
- Soy sauce mixing condiment
- Flavor accent for noodles 🍜
- Modern fusion sauces

---

## 🧬 Composition Notes

Depending on quality:

### High-end (real wasabi-based)
- Wasabi japonica root
- Natural fibers
- Mild heat with herbal notes

### Common commercial version
- Horseradish
- Mustard powder
- Starch
- Green coloring

## ⚗️ Activation Protocol

1. Mix powder with small amount of water
2. Wait 30–60 seconds for flavor activation
3. Do NOT inhale deeply during preparation (safety advisory)
