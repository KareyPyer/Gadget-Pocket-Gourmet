# 🍯 Teriyaki Sauce (照り焼きソース)

> *“Gloss, balance, and controlled sweetness — the art of edible shine.” — Dr Umami*

---

## 🧬 Identity

- **Japanese Name:** 照り焼きソース / 照り焼きのたれ
- **Romanization:** Teriyaki sauce
- **Category:** Sauce
- **Origin:** Japan (modern standardized version popularized globally)
- **Flavor Profile:** Sweet, salty, glossy umami, slightly caramelized

---

## 🧪 Description

Teriyaki sauce is a sweet-savory glaze traditionally made from soy sauce, mirin, and sugar.

The term **“teriyaki”** comes from:
- **Teri (照り)** → shine / glaze
- **Yaki (焼き)** → grilled or broiled

Dr Umami classifies it as:

> “A biochemical transformation that turns protein into shiny happiness.”

---

## 🍜 Traditional Uses

- Grilled chicken (teriyaki chicken)
- Salmon glaze
- Stir-fried vegetables
- Meat marinades
- Rice bowl toppings (donburi)

---

## 🧬 Core Ingredients

- Shoyu (soy sauce)
- Mirin (sweet rice wine)
- Sugar or honey
- Sake (optional)
- Garlic or ginger (modern variations)
