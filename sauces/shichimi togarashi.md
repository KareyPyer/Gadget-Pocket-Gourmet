> *“Seven ingredients. Infinite possibilities.” — Dr Umami*

---

## 🧬 Identity

- **Japanese Name:** 七味唐辛子
- **Romanization:** Shichimi Tōgarashi
- **Category:** Spice Blend
- **Origin:** Edo-period Japan
- **Flavor Profile:** Spicy, citrusy, nutty, aromatic complexity

---

## 🧪 Description

Shichimi togarashi is a traditional Japanese spice mix composed of seven ingredients, typically including chili pepper, sansho pepper, citrus peel, sesame seeds, hemp seeds, ginger, and nori.

---

## 🍜 Traditional Uses

- Ramen seasoning
- Udon enhancement
- Yakitori topping
- Rice seasoning
- Soups and broths

---

## 🧬 Common Ingredients

- Chili pepper
- Sansho pepper
- Orange peel
- Black sesame
- White sesame
- Ginger
- Nori (seaweed)
