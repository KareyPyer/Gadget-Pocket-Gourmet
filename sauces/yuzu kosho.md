# 🌶️ Yuzu Kosho (柚子胡椒)

> *“Small jar. Maximum chaos.” — Dr Umami*

---

## 🧬 Identity

- **Japanese Name:** 柚子胡椒
- **Romanization:** Yuzu-koshō
- **Category:** Condiment / Paste
- **Origin:** Kyushu, Japan
- **Flavor Profile:** Spicy, citrusy, salty, aromatic

---

## 🧪 Description

Yuzu kosho is a fermented paste made from:

- Yuzu citrus peel
- Chili peppers
- Salt

Despite its name containing “pepper,” it contains no black pepper.

---

## 🍜 Traditional Uses

- Grilled meat seasoning
- Ramen spice enhancer
- Hot pot dipping paste
- Udon flavor boost
- Sushi fusion condiment

---

## 🧬 Variants

- Green Yuzu Kosho (unripe chili, sharper taste)
- Red Yuzu Kosho (ripe chili, deeper heat)
